Questions and Answers

1. Variables for Forecasting Churn

To predict churn effectively, the following variables are considered:

  • Daily Active Time: Indicates engagement; lower activity signals churn risk.
  • Number of Daily Sessions: Higher sessions mean deeper involvement.
  • Time Since Last Login: Longer gaps suggest declining interest.
  • Lifetime Value (Tier): High-value users may churn differently.
  • Platform: User behavior varies by platform.

Example: A user with 5 sessions/day is less likely to churn than one with 1 session/week.
